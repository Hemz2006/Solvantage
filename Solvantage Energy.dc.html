Who we are

Solvantage Energy Systems Private Limited is a Hyderabad-based solar power company operating a 2 MW generation plant near the city. We design, build, and operate high-efficiency solar assets for private and government enterprises.

What sets us apart is simple: we are entirely women-led. Our engineers, operators, and project leads bring precision and accountability to every megawatt we deliver — proving that clean energy and inclusive leadership go hand in hand.
